SERVICES

TAILORING & ALTERATIONS

Anatoly’s has been in business since 1995 and we pride
ourselves on our high end European style of tailoring. We
offer alterations for both men and women in formal traditional
garments as well as casual wear.

MADE TO MEASURE

Made to measure is a custom garment based on our
exclusive fitting block also known as our try-on. With this try-
on we use 35 distinct measurements to ensure you are
designing a garment with a remarkable fit!

BESPOKE

Bespoke is building your suit or garment from the floor up

CUSTOM DENIM

Why made to order jeans? Many of the mass
produced denim on the market is not only ill-fitting, but also
lacks in quality of cotton. At Anatoly's we pride ourselves in
delivering a quality denim product that you can wear for
years to come.

MADE TO ORDER

Small batch production products, orders based on size sets
so you know they fit, design it your way!
